About Me

Hi there! I'm Anthony Em, a software developer, sci-fi and thriller writer with a passion for technology and exploration. My journey has taken me to reside in at least five countries, instilling a deep appreciation for languages and diverse cultures.

With a foundation in technical expertise, I excel in clear communication, drawing from my experience teaching languages. Each project is an exciting puzzle, an opportunity to craft accessible and intuitive technology.

Philosophy & Passion

My work is driven by a philosophy that tech should not only function but also inspire and engage. My passion for the cosmos and life's big questions informs my creative and inquisitive nature.

Whether I'm learning new languages or exploring tech and cosmic concepts, I'm always in the company of my curious cats, whose agility inspires my approach to innovation. Together, we navigate the vastness of technology and space, dedicated to uncovering new horizons.
